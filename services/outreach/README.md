# Outreach FlowRunner Extension

Connect FlowRunner to [Outreach](https://www.outreach.io/) to automate sales engagement. The
extension uses OAuth2 and covers prospects, accounts, sequence enrollment, tasks, calls,
opportunities, templates, and snippets - plus real-time webhook triggers.

## Ideal Use Cases

- Add captured leads as prospects, then enroll them in a sequence.
- Keep accounts and prospects in sync with your CRM or data sources.
- Tag and segment prospects automatically as they move through your funnel.
- Queue follow-up tasks for reps and mark them complete from a flow.
- Log calls from an external dialer into a prospect's timeline.
- Create and update opportunities to keep the pipeline current.
- Manage reusable email templates and snippets for consistent messaging.
- React in real time to prospect, account, task, opportunity, call, and mailing events.

## Agent Ideas

- On a new prospect, enrich it from another source, then enroll it in the right sequence and assign an owner.
- When an inbound lead is captured, find-or-create its account and link the prospect before starting outreach.
- On task completion, log the outcome elsewhere and queue the next follow-up task automatically.
- When an opportunity changes stage, notify sales leadership and update a pipeline dashboard.
- Nightly, list prospects filtered by email or tag and generate an engagement report from their mailings and calls.
